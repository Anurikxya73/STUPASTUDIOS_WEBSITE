About STUPA STUDIO

STUPA STUDIO is an independent creative and technology studio founded by Rijan Koirala. We specialize in crafting original games, apps, and digital solutions that combine design, innovation, and functionality.

From concept to code, from prototypes to polished products — we handle every step with passion and precision. Beyond our own titles, we also collaborate with clients and brands to deliver custom software, websites, and interactive experiences tailored to their vision.

At STUPA STUDIO, we believe that every project — whether a game or an app — should tell a story, spark creativity, and connect with people. Our mission is simple: build experiences that inspire and endure.
